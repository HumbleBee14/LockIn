# Privacy Policy

LockIn is designed to keep your information on your own Machine.

## What LockIn stores

LockIn saves your schedules, block sets, and settings locally on your device so it can enforce the blocks you set up. This stays on your Machine.

## What LockIn does not do

- No analytics, telemetry, or usage tracking
- No accounts, no sign-in
- LockIn does not send your schedules, block lists, browsing activity, or any personal data to us or to any third party

## Network use

LockIn connects to the internet only when you ask it to — for example, when you import a public blocklist by URL, or to check the current time from a trusted source to keep blocks accurate. It does not transmit your data in the process.

## Contact

Questions about privacy can be raised as an issue on the project's repository.
